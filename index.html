About Me

Hello and welcome to my portfolio page. My name is Sean Gowing. I have a multitude of life experience and decided that web development was going to be my next new frontier

Prior to doing web development I was a US Marine for 7 years. I did one deployment to Iraq in 2004 in support of Operation Iraqi Freedom. One thing I can say about Iraq was "It's hot!!!!" The hottest day, while we were there, was 135 degrees

After getting out of the Marine Corps I was a Police Officer for 15 years. Due to a back injury, I was forced to retire at 38 years old. This brought me to my next new career in programming and web development. I really enjoy working with computers and have been using since 386's came out.

This portfolio will be a work in progress for me and I hope you enjoy seeing the projects that I am working on.
